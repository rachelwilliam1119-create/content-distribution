Everything You Need to Know About Wet Transoms

“My boat has a wet transom. What does it mean?” This is a very common question we receive from our customers. The transom is the rearmost vertical structure on the boat. Most modern fibreglass boats have a transom capable of holding one or two engines, outboard or inboard motors.

Read the full article to learn what it means, how to assess & fix it, and the problems you may face if you have a rotten transom.
